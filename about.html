About This Project

The Concept

A year-long biodiversity documetation of 1m² area in Gundmi, India.

This project documents every insect, fungus, and other creatures found within a fixed 1 meter × 1 meter plot of land over the course of one year. By observing this tiny patch of earth through all seasons, we may discover the remarkable biodiversity that exists in even the smallest spaces. Project began on 1st of January, 2026.

Methodology

  • A fixed 1m × 1m plot has been marked in Gundmi, Karnataka, India.
  • The plot is visited regularly, (almost) everyday throughout the year
  • All visible life forms are photographed and documented
  • Weather conditions and moon phases are recorded for each sighting
  • Identifications are made to the best of ability, with scientific names when known
  • The patch will not be intentionally disturbed

Location

The study plot is located in Gundmi, Karnataka, India..

Timezone: Asia/Kolkata

Seasons

The local seasons are defined as:

  • Winter: Months december, january, february
  • Summer: Months march, april, may
  • Monsoon: Months june, july, august, september
  • Post-Monsoon: Months october, november

Categories

Sightings are organized into these categories:

  • Insect
  • Arachnid
  • Amphibian
  • Fungus
  • Mollusk
  • Other
