Rules

The constraints I follow for this project:

  • A fixed 1m × 1m plot has been marked in Gundmi, Karnataka, India.
  • The plot is visited regularly, (almost) everyday throughout the year
  • All visible life forms are photographed and documented
  • Weather conditions and moon phases are recorded for each sighting
  • Identifications are made to the best of ability, with scientific names when known

Non-interference

The integrity of this project depends on observing without altering. I follow these principles:

  • I never step into the square meter area
  • I never force a bug into a pose or move it for a better shot
  • I never interfere with the plot: no watering, no handling, no modifying
  • If a creature seems affected by my presence, I back away

The goal is to be as passive as possible. I know it's not fully achievable. Bugs see me and they will react. But I try my best to observe without disturbing what I came to witness.

Species Identification

The usefulness of this project depends on right identification of the species.

  • I aim to get as accurate as possible with the help of lovely community at iNaturalist, technology like Google Lens, and LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini
  • I add images that are clear enough to identify the Order / Family / Genus or Species. If the images are not good enough, then I discard them.
  • Mistakes are inevitable, some of the identifications may be retroactively corrected.

The goal is to be as accurate as possible. I am learning on the job.

Seasons

The local seasons are defined as:

  • Winter: Months december, january, february
  • Summer: Months march, april, may
  • Monsoon: Months june, july, august, september
  • Post-Monsoon: Months october, november

Categories

Sightings are organized into these categories:

  • Insect
  • Arachnid
  • Amphibian
  • Plant
  • Fungus
  • Mollusk
  • Other
