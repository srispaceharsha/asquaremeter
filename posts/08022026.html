Week 6: It feels like summer

February 08, 2026

Somedays I see many bugs, other days very few.

Hello!

This square meter plot is drying and fast. The daytime temperature is rising, the Philippine Spinach has turned yellow.

I saw fewer bugs in the last few days, I suspect the trend might continue into summer.

It has just been a few weeks, and the data of the species is already a bit messy. Double counting, mis-identified bugs, same bug logged with genus name as well as family name etc.

I will fix it in the coming weeks.

’Til next week!
