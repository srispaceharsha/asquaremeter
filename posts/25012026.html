Week 4: Dust on the sensor

January 18, 2026

I see bugs in my dreams now.

Hello!

The ant-mimicking spiders have been the highlight of this week(one, two and three). I saw three different varieties, and each time they fooled me for a few moments. There are incredible theories about how they evolved into such convincing mimics.

I stepped out at night a few times this week with a headlamp to observe the plot. Night photography is really hard without the right equipment, but spotting the paddy field frog was quite nice.

The plot’s undergrowth is drying up, and I can now more or less see the soil easily, unlike the last few weeks.

Another unexpected thing is that the plot is becoming an island of greenery because of all my movement around it. The immediate surrounding has become dry. I can’t think of a way to avoid this for now.

’Til next week!
