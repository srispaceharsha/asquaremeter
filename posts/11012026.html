Week 2: Ants marching

January 11, 2026

I have started talking to bugs.

Hello!

Did you know that ants do not trigger the touch-me-not plant’s defense mechanism? So cool!

Well, would you look at that stats page, so many numbers! I think I have documented quite a lot of “resident” bugs, and now the number of new species I encounter might start to decrease.

If you are planning to start a similar project, make sure you do not pick a plot right below a coconut tree. The plot I selected, however, is right beneath one. The tree’s branches look like they are ready to fall at any moment. So, this bug documentation project has turned into an adventure sport!

Note to self: Be graceful in your movements. Do not spook the bugs.

’Til next week!
