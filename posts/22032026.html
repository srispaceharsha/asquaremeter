Week 12: life outside the square

March 22, 2026

The summer heat gets to you.

Hello!

The ghost ants (Tapinoma melanocephalum) have taken over the square meter plot. They are everywhere. They are tiny, barely visible and they move really fast. I think this is the reason the acrobat ants have moved out!

Life outside the square meter is still a bit hectic. I couldn't spend more than 4-5 minutes at a time to observe the plot.

But spending time with this hoverfly was fun.

You guessed right, it can hover in the air!

And They seem friendly and aren't easily spooked by my presence.

Also, I haven't seen the usual spiders (Lynx spiders, jumping spiders) in a while. Hah.

’Til next week!
