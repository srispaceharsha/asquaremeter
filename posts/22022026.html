Week 8: Game of life (and death)

February 22, 2026

Kill or be killed, that seems to be the vibe.

Hello!

Spiders. I find them very fascinating. Eight eyes, eight legs, venom, ability to jump, and they can make silky webs! Evolution surely optimized these critters alright.

I've been observing a particular type of spider named "Crab spider" from Thomisidae family. There are many of them here on the square meter plot. I see them regularly. It is hard to spot them if they aren't on the move.

They seem to really like to hunt the carpenter ants. On one particular night, I saw 4 of them having these ants for dinner.

I made a short video explaining what I learnt. Go, watch it!

’Til next week!
