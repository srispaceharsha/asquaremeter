Week 4: Dust on the sensor

February 01, 2026

We are just bugs with opposable thumbs.

Hello!

This square meter plot is a battleground of life. I see relentless persuit of survival; each bug for its own. Brutal. Each adopted evolutionarily to be specialist in certain ways, to be effient hunter.

Well, a month went by, and here we are. I have already seen a lot. Courting, mating, caring, and killing.

Circle of life, eh?

’Til next week!
