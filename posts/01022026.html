Week 5: A mirror of life

February 01, 2026

Staring into the eyes of a tiny spider is quite haunting.

Hello!

This square meter plot is a battleground of life. I see relentless pursuit of survival; each bug for its own. Brutal. Each adopted evolutionarily to be specialist in certain ways, to be efficient hunter. Poetic.

Well, a month went by, and here we are. I have already seen a lot. Courting, mating, caring, and killing.

Circle of life, eh?

’Til next week!
