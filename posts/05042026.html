Week 14: Termites

March 29, 2026

Everything decays, into something else

Hello!

Another week went by! Time sure moves so quickly these days.

Termites have taken over the wooden poles I planted to mark the four corners of the plot, as well as the block of wood I've kept inside the plot. They work quite fast. They covered 3 of the poles with mud overnight.

I spotted something interesting on the leaf of an asthma plant on the edge of the plot. This looks like some sort of egg. It took a while to find anything similar online.

I saw very similar thing on this page, and a similar match on iNaturalist listing. My complaint is most of these description do not give an idea of the size of the bug, egg or the creature.

I'll keep a close eye on this thing and see what comes out of it, if at all.

’Til next week!
