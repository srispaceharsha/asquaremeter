Week 1: Not bad!

January 04, 2026

My knees hurt.

Hello!

So, end of the week (well, just 4 days)! 20 species found, from very tiny to slightly bigger ones!

I saw 3 new hoppers that I hadn't seen before. They are tiny!

So, the morning light is not enough to get good quality photos, I have to bump up ISO, which leads to very grainy images. Looks like I will spend monies on a flash. Sigh.

My macro photography skills have rusted. I am hoping it will get better as the time goes on.

I also discoverd a very similar project done before by Chris Helzer. Read about it here. The photographs he has taken are very clean and precise! Now I have a benchmark to aim for!

One week at a time!
