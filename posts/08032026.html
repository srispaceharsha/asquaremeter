Week 10: Warm days

March 08, 2026

Lack of bugs is information too

Hello!

Afternoon temperatures are hovering around 32–33 °C these days, and summer has only just begun. The Monsoon rains likely won’t arrive until mid-May.

I have barely observed any new species this week, the Three-striped Lady Beetle is nice surprise.

The patch is drying up.

This might be the first weather-related change in insect density in the square meter patch. These days, it’s mostly ants.

I don’t know whether there are fewer bugs because it’s summer or because I’m paying less attention. I'll never know.

’Til next week!
