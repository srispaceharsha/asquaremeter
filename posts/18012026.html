Oh it's a bug's life for me

Hello!

The flies from Tephritidae family have a funky dating process. Check it out here. I had to push myself a bit to record all of the footage over 3.5 hours. It was quite fascinating to see the whole thing happening.

Otherwise, the patch is still green, and quite a few varieties of bugs are still around. I think I have seen five or six different species that I haven't been able to get a decent picture of yet. So, fingers crossed!

’Til next week!
